Commercial Disinfection Services

In response to the Covid-19 pandemic and in an attempt to provide the highest quality services to our customers, we have placed a new emphasis on deep disinfection. Our goal is to ensure keep our community as clean and safe as possible. In order to achieve this goal, we are providing enhanced services which include: High Heat Carpet Cleaning. Our new high heat carpet cleaning method has been tested and proven to kill more bacteria, viruses and fungi than other carpet cleaning services. The service also provides superior results compared to other carpet cleaning methods. Frequent cleaning with this new method will not only keep your customers safe but will also prolong the life of your carpets, postponing replacement costs.

Disinfection & Fogging Services

Our new fogging disinfection service is a full disinfection solution which fully disinfects areas and surfaces that can be otherwise difficult to reach. We are committed to do our part to provide the best disinfection services. It is our hope that these services will be part of slowing the further spread of bacteria, viruses and other pathogens.
